7  <title><command>pg_receivexlog</command> renamed to <command>pg_receivewal</command></title>
8
9   <indexterm>
10     <primary>pg_receivexlog</primary>
11     <see>pg_receivewal</see>
12   </indexterm>
13
14   <para>
15    PostgreSQL 9.6 and below provided a command named
16    <command>pg_receivexlog</command>
17    <indexterm><primary>pg_receivexlog</primary></indexterm>
18    to fetch write-ahead-log (WAL) files.  This command was renamed to <command>pg_receivewal</command>, see
19    <xref linkend="app-pgreceivewal"/> for documentation of <command>pg_receivewal</command> and see
20    <link linkend="release-prior">the release notes for PostgreSQL 10</link> for details
21    on this change.
22   </para>
