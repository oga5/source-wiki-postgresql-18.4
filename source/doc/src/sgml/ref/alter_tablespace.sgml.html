17 <refnamediv>
18  <refname>ALTER TABLESPACE</refname>
19  <refpurpose>change the definition of a tablespace</refpurpose>
20 </refnamediv>
21
22 <refsynopsisdiv>
23<synopsis>
24ALTER TABLESPACE <replaceable>name</replaceable> RENAME TO <replaceable>new_name</replaceable>
25ALTER TABLESPACE <replaceable>name</replaceable> OWNER TO { <replaceable>new_owner</replaceable> | CURRENT_ROLE | CURRENT_USER | SESSION_USER }

28</synopsis>

31 <refsect1>
32  <title>Description</title>
33
34  <para>
35   <command>ALTER TABLESPACE</command> can be used to change the definition of
36   a tablespace.
37  </para>
38
39  <para>
40   You must own the tablespace to change the definition of a tablespace.
41   To alter the owner, you must also be able to <literal>SET ROLE</literal>
42   to the new owning role.
43   (Note that superusers have these privileges automatically.)
44  </para>
45
46 </refsect1>

48 <refsect1>
49  <title>Parameters</title>
50
51  <variablelist>
52   <varlistentry>
53    <term><replaceable class="parameter">name</replaceable></term>
54    <listitem>
55     <para>
56      The name of an existing tablespace.
57     </para>
58    </listitem>
59   </varlistentry>
60
61   <varlistentry>
62    <term><replaceable class="parameter">new_name</replaceable></term>
63    <listitem>
64     <para>
65      The new name of the tablespace.  The new name cannot
66      begin with <literal>pg_</literal>, as such names
67      are reserved for system tablespaces.
68     </para>
69    </listitem>
70   </varlistentry>
71
72   <varlistentry>
73    <term><replaceable class="parameter">new_owner</replaceable></term>
74    <listitem>
75     <para>
76      The new owner of the tablespace.
77     </para>
78    </listitem>
79   </varlistentry>
80
81   <varlistentry>
82    <term><replaceable class="parameter">tablespace_option</replaceable></term>
83    <listitem>
84     <para>
85      A tablespace parameter to be set or reset.  Currently, the only
86      available parameters are <varname>seq_page_cost</varname>,
87      <varname>random_page_cost</varname>, <varname>effective_io_concurrency</varname>
88      and <varname>maintenance_io_concurrency</varname>.
89      Setting these values for a particular tablespace will override the
90      planner's usual estimate of the cost of reading pages from tables in
91      that tablespace, and how many concurrent I/Os are issued, as established
92      by the configuration parameters of the
93      same name (see <xref linkend="guc-seq-page-cost"/>,
94      <xref linkend="guc-random-page-cost"/>,
95      <xref linkend="guc-effective-io-concurrency"/>,
96      <xref linkend="guc-maintenance-io-concurrency"/>).  This may be useful if
97      one tablespace is located on a disk which is faster or slower than the
98      remainder of the I/O subsystem.
99     </para>
100    </listitem>
101   </varlistentry>
102
103  </variablelist>
104 </refsect1>

106 <refsect1>
107  <title>Examples</title>
108
109  <para>
110   Rename tablespace <literal>index_space</literal> to <literal>fast_raid</literal>:
111<programlisting>
112ALTER TABLESPACE index_space RENAME TO fast_raid;
113</programlisting>
114  </para>
115
116  <para>
117   Change the owner of tablespace <literal>index_space</literal>:
118<programlisting>
119ALTER TABLESPACE index_space OWNER TO mary;
120</programlisting></para>
121 </refsect1>

124  <title>Compatibility</title>
125
126  <para>
127   There is no <command>ALTER TABLESPACE</command> statement in
128   the SQL standard.
129  </para>
