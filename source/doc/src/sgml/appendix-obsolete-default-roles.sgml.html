6<sect1 id="default-roles" xreflabel="default-roles">
7  <title>Default Roles Renamed to Predefined Roles</title>
8
9   <indexterm>
10     <primary>default-roles</primary>
11   </indexterm>
12
13   <para>
14    PostgreSQL 13 and below used the term <quote>Default Roles</quote>.  However, as these
15    roles are not able to actually be changed and are installed as part of the
16    system at initialization time, the more appropriate term to use is <quote>Predefined Roles</quote>.
17    See <xref linkend="predefined-roles"/> for current documentation regarding
18    Predefined Roles, and <link linkend="release-prior">the release notes for
19    PostgreSQL 14</link> for details on this change.
20   </para>
21
22</sect1>
